Flask 10 inch slim white (2 rings) connection 1/4 'Organic

Model: WH-6014WW-04N

Price:

334.80 UAH (9.00 $)

The 10 inch slim Organic flask is a replaceable housing for household filters and reverse osmosis systems. Made of durable white plastic. White flasks are used as second and/or third stages of filtration for carbon cartridges or softening. On cartridges of this type, sealing rings are installed on both sides. When installed in a flask, the cartridge is fixed at the top and bottom. It has compactness, reliability, durability and resistance to differences in main pressure. Consists of 2 main parts: white cover with plastic thread 1/4 inch, white flask with two rings of seals. Provided for a large range of cartridges.

The Slim 10 Organic flask filter works according to the following principle: water under pressure enters the flask body, passes through the cartridge and the cleaned one moves to the next filtration stage or to the outside of the filter. The life of the cartridge depends on the degree of contamination and the amount of filtered water. Any cartridge, regardless of its type, has a certain resource, after the development of which it is necessary to make a replacement. Unscrew the flask with a key, remove the old cartridge and rinse the flask with water. Insert a new cartridge, screw the housing to the cover until it stops.

Characteristics:

  • Body color: white
  • Internal thread connection: 1/4'
  • Operating pressure: 7 bar
  • Operating temperature: 2 - 45 ° C
  • Cartridge type: 10 slim
