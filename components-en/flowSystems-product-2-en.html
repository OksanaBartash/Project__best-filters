FILTRONS Standart (softening P5 + CBC + UM, tap drop) provides universal cleaning of tap water from mechanical impurities (rust, sludge, sand, etc.), as well as dissolved impurities, such as free chlorine, organochlorine compounds, pesticides, petroleum products, heavy metals and other organic and inorganic substances. Eliminates unpleasant odors and improves the taste of water. Due to the presence of the cartridge with ion-exchange resin softens the water.

Cleaning is carried out in 3 stages:

  • 1st stage - mechanical cleaning is carried out with a cartridge made of foamed polypropylene, the filtration degree is 5 microns. Removes rust, sludge particles, clay, sand, microorganisms, fibers and other impurities of organic and inorganic origin. Resource: approximately 6000 liters.
  • 2nd degree - softening, water purification: removal of hardness salts (calcium and magnesium), partially - iron. The softening cartridge is filled with ion exchange resin. Due to which there is a replacement of hardness ions Ca2 + and Mg2 +, as well as (partially) dissolved ferrous iron - Fe2 + with sodium ions Na +. These salts are the cause of the formation of scale on the devices, as well as the heating of water. In addition, dissolved iron is the cause of the unpleasant smell and taste of water. Resource: approximately 6 months.
  • 3rd degree - purification of water from chlorine, organic compounds, odors, flavors. The activated carbon cartridge of coconut shells removes chlorine, organic and organochlorine substances from the water. It has an increased possibility of adsorption of organic compounds and free chlorine, removes color, odor. Resource: approximately 6 months.

The resource of replaceable cartridges and reverse osmosis membrane is given approximately and depends on the quality of the source water.

Characteristics:

  • Filter type: flow-through
  • Productivity: 2-4 l/min
  • Cartridge type: 10 slim
  • Cleaning: Mechanical impurities, chlorine, hardness, hydrogen sulfide, iron
