  • 1st - mechanical cleaning cartridge Filtrons 5 microns, which removes small debris, sand, rust from the water.

Contamination of the surface of the filtering membrane is irreversible and leads to a complete stop of the water purification process. Therefore, a preliminary three-stage filtration of incoming water is used, and the membrane is organized so that all contaminants entering it do not settle on its surface, but are washed into the sewer.
  • 5th - Filtrons postfilter, which improves the taste and smell of water after the tank.

Stages of cleaning:

  1. Mechanical polypropylene fiber cartridge with a filtration rating of 5 μm. The mechanical filter is used for pre-treatment of water in front of the membrane element.

  4. Membrane element. A semi-permeable membrane with pores of 0.8-1.2 nanometers purifies water from bacteria, viruses, heavy metals by 95-98% and discharges them into the sewer with part of the discharged water.
  5. Postcarbon. Water enters the last stage of purification - a carbon filter to improve the organoleptic qualities of water.

For correct and long-term operation of the reverse osmosis system, it is necessary to replace the filter elements in time. The first three cartridges should be replaced at least every 6 months, the membrane element serves up to 3 years and depends on the quality of the pre-treatment of the incoming water, postcarbon - once a year.

The storage capacity of the filter can be positioned both vertically and horizontally.
