Cabinet-type water softening systems are designed for domestic use in cases where the installation site is limited. In cabinet-type filters, the filter housing and the salt-solvent tank are combined in one unit. They are used in apartments, private houses, cafes, restaurants and so on. Purpose: designed to eliminate the hardness salts of calcium and magnesium from water. Softening of water occurs by ion exchange. Suitable for cleaning drinking and service water from underground sources or city water supply. Cation exchange resin is used for softening (filter material service life is up to 7 years).

The water softening system is designed to purify water from high content:

  • - hardness salts;
  • - iron (up to 0.9 mg/)

The salt solution is passed through the filter material, and the removed calcium and magnesium salts are washed into the sewer.

  • Operating pressure: 2 - 6 bar

  • Regeneration method: salt washing
  • System base color: white cover, white base

Water softening system Lucy 850SE-Maxi is a compact cabinet-type filter with a chlorinator, focused on removing hardness salts (calcium and magnesium). Calcium and magnesium ions are exchanged for an equivalent amount of sodium ions, resulting in a decrease in rigidity without significant changes in the total salt content of water. The filter protects water heating elements from the formation of scale and protects household appliances from premature breakdowns. You will also save up to 50% of detergents and washing powder, because soft water foams much better. Included resin - 25 liters. Suitable for cleaning drinking and technical water from underground sources or city water supply. The filter has a compact size, so it can be installed in any small room.
