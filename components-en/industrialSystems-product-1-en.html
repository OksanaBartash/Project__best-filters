The complex water purification system 1054 Clack CC is a modern production system that allows you to simultaneously remove hardness salts (calcium and magnesium) and ions of iron, manganese, etc. For cleaning, the Filtrons X1 multimedia ion exchange material of complex action is used: the life of the filter material is 6-10 years (depends on the source water and operating conditions).

Filter material Filtrons X1 is a multimedia ion-exchange material of complex action, which during operation simultaneously reduces color, oxidizability, removes ions of iron, manganese. Calcium and magnesium are exchanged for an equivalent number of sodium ions, resulting in a decrease in the overall hardness without significant change in the overall mineralization of water. During operation, the components are "delaminated," depending on the bulk weight and density of the filter materials, which each perform their own function. Loading Filtrons X1 requires periodic cleaning and restoration of its properties (regeneration).

The regeneration process occurs periodically after the passage of the calculated volume of water. Regeneration is carried out in several successive stages, namely, reverse washing, salting, direct washing, preparation of a solution for the next regeneration. Tableted table salt is used. Thanks to the use of the Clack CC control valve, the regeneration process is automatic.

The Clack CC premium control valve provides efficient water purification in automatic mode. The valve is individually programmed according to the quality of the source water and the customer's needs. The program monitors the filtration process, water flow statistics and provides the regeneration process. The control valve screen displays information about all current processes and service and alarm messages.
